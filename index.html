Avengers: Age of Ultron

Released: 1 May 2015

Starring

  • Actor
  • Robert Downey Jr. as Tony Stark / Iron Man
  • Chris Hemsworth as Thor
  • Mark Ruffalo as Bruce Banner / Hulk
  • Chris Evans as Steve Rogers / Captain America
  • Scarlett Johansson as Natasha Romanoff / Black Widow
  • Jeremy Renner as Clint Barton / Hawkeye
  • James Spader as Ultron
  • Samuel L. Jackson as Nick Fury

Movie Quote

Steve Rogers: You get hurt, hurt 'em back. You get killed... walk it off.

Plot

Tony Stark starts creating a peacekeeping program called Ultron based after his home AI Jarvis. Ultron quickly gains conciseness and turns against humanity and the Avengers’. Now it’s up to the Avenger’s to stop Ultron before he destroys the humankind.
